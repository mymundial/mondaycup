Updated share/export commentary triggers

Tier bands
- Elite: TEAM_RANK 1-10
- Strong: TEAM_RANK 11-24
- Underdog: TEAM_RANK 25-48

Selection behaviour
- Commentary is deterministic, not random. The same match state returns the same line.
- Priority order: exceptional event > upset/giant-killing > group status > qualification result > knockout round > generic fallback.

Group stage special triggers
- Underdog beats Elite: A GIANT HAS FALLEN / THE WORLD JUST TOOK NOTICE / THEY NEVER SAW YOU COMING
- Elite loses to Underdog: THE NATION DEMANDS ANSWERS / A DISASTER UNFOLDS / HEADLINES WRITE THEMSELVES
- Perfect group, 9 points: THREE GAMES. THREE WINS. / PERFECTION. / THE TEAM TO BEAT.
- Qualify despite losing match 3: DEFEAT NEVER FELT SO GOOD / SOMEONE CHECK THE RULEBOOK / YOU'LL TAKE THAT
- Eliminated narrowly on points/GD: MILLIMETRES FROM GLORY / ONE GOAL CHANGED EVERYTHING / SO CLOSE IT HURTS
